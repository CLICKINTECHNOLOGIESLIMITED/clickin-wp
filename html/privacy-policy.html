CLICKIN PRIVACY POLICY – JULY 2014

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how information about you or associated with you (“Personal Information” is collected, used and disclosed by Clickin Technologies Limited when you use access and use our mobile application, Clickin’.

Our policy applies to visitors, users and others who access the service (“Users”). Our privacy policy may be modified from time-to-time. We will alert you that changes have been made by indicating on our privacy policy the date it was last updated. If we make any material changes we will notify you by email or by means of a notice on this web site prior to the change becoming effective. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

1. Information you directly provide us:

Registration and Profile Information: When you sign-up on Clickin’ we will ask for your phone number, name, email address, date of birth and password. You may also choose to provide us with additional information such as your profile picture, gender and location.

Finding Friends on Clickin’: If you choose you can use the “Find Friends” feature to locate other people with Clickin accounts either through (i) your contact list (ii) third-party social media sites. If you choose to find friends through your devices contact list, Clickin’ will access your contact list to determine whether or not someone associated with your contact is using Clickin’. If you choose to find friends through a third party social media site, then you will be prompted to set up a link to that third-party service and you understand that any information that such service may provide us will be governed by this privacy policy.

Adding a Partner on Clickin’: You can add or invite a partner to connect with on Clickin’ by providing their phone number. We use this information to send a request to the selected partner, which he or she can choose to accept or reject.

Communications: We receive and store the messages you receive and send with other users that you have made a connection with. We will also store any communication between you and Clickin’ relating to out services.

Information you post: We collect information on your actions that you take when interacting with content posted by others users of Clickin’ such as posts that you have commented on, posts that you like starred etc.

2. Information We Collect Automatically When You Use the Services

When you access or use Clickin’, we automatically collect information about you, including:

Log Information: We log information about your use of our websites, including your browser type and language, access times, pages viewed, your IP address and the website you visited before navigating to our websites.

Device Information: We may collect information about the computer or device you use to access our Services, including the hardware model, operating system and version, MAC address, unique device identifier, phone number, International Mobile Equipment Identity ("IMEI") and mobile network information. In addition, the Services may access your device's native phonebook and image storage applications, with your consent, to facilitate your use of certain features of the Services.

Information Collected by Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: We use various technologies to collect information, and this may include sending cookies to you. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us to improve our Services and your experience, see which areas and features of our Services are popular and count visits. We may also collect information using web beacons (also known as "tracking pixels").

HOW DO WE USE THE INFORMATION THAT WE COLLECT?

We use the information collected to enable us to:

  • Provide Clickin’
  • Create your account and identify you as a user
  • To help you find and connect with your friends, including inviting them to Clickin’
  • To develop, deliver, measure, analyze and improve Clickin’ and develop new products and features.
  • To respond to queries and emails
  • Send you administrative and service related communications
  • To send you newsletter, promotions, upcoming events and other news about product and services offered by Clickin’
  • To detect and prevent abusive, fraudulent, malicious or potentially illegal activities and to protect the rights, safety and property of Clickin’s users.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE SHARE WITH THIRD PARTIES?

Sharing with other users

  1. The information displayed on your profile will be accessible by all users of Clickin’. Subject to the information provided by you, this could include your Name, Display Picture, Age, Gender, Location, list of people that are following you and the list of people you are following.
  2. The visibility of your list of partners on Clickin’ on your profile page is determined a visibility setting defined by you. You partners may choose to make their relationship with you visible or private to visitors of their profile page.
  3. Content such as messages, images, videos, etc. that you choose to share with your partners on Clickin’ is solely accessible to both you and the respective partner.
  4. Any content that you and your partner deliberately post to The Feed becomes visible to both your respective list of followers on Clickin’. No content mentioning you can be posted The Feed without your prior consent. Posts shared on Clickin’ can also be synced to be posted on with other social networking websites and apps
  5. Your comments and stars on content posted on the feed.

Service Providers and Other Third Parties

We will share the information provided by you with third parties to help us provide and improve Clickin’. These services providers, such as analytics or hosting providers, have access to your personal information only to perform services on our behalf and are obligated not disclose it or use it for any other purposes. We are not responsible for the actions of third parties, nor are we responsible for any additional information you provide directly to any third parties.

We will also share your information the following circumstances:

  • When you give us consent to do so
  • When we believe in good faith that that such disclosure is required to comply with the law or legal processes or respond to lawful requests, including responding to lawful subpoenas, warrants or court orders.
  • In connection with a merger or acquisition of the Company’s assets, financing or acquisition, or in any other situation where personal information may be disclosed or transferred as a business assets.

LINKS TO OTHER SITES

When you click on a link to any other website or location, you will leave Clickin’ and go to another site and another entity may collect Personal Information or anonymous data from you. We have no control over, do not review, and cannot be responsible for, these outside websites or their content. Please be aware that the terms of this Privacy Policy do not apply to these outside websites or content, or to any collection of data after you click on links to such outside websites.

Our security practices and procedures limit access to personal information on need to know basis only.

Such measures may include, where appropriate, the use of firewalls, secure server facilities, implementing proper access rights management systems and processes, careful selection of processors, standard authentication and encryption policies for our servers and other technically and commercially reasonable measures.

However, Clickin Technologies Limited cannot guarantee in any way with respect to any Personally Identifiable Information or Contents that You upload transmit, share or display through the Application or the Site and You do so at Your own risk.
